This is the first post on my new blog. I built this site with Astro, Tailwind CSS, and a strict monochrome design philosophy.

## Why Astro?

Astro generates static HTML by default, which means fast load times and excellent performance. No unnecessary JavaScript shipped to the browser.

## Design Principles

- **Monochrome only:** Black, grey, and white. No accent colors.
- **Typography first:** Let the content breathe through generous spacing and careful type hierarchy.
- **No clutter:** No tracking, no analytics, no comments. Just words.
