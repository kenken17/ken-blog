Saw a lot of you-tuber spitting out words into AI agent like magic spell. It impressed my just like when the first time saw Ironman talking to Jarvis. I must give it a try!

A simple google/gpt-ing lead me to [Handy](https://handy.computer/), since I am on linux (Pop! OS), my choice is limited. When I first installed, i cant really get the key combo working at all. Then I found out mt Pop! OS is on Wayland which turned off the Global Shortcut for `security` reason. Turn on the X11 application compatibility in your settings, i.e. **All keys**, before you can use the application successfully.

![X11 Compatibility](/images/2026-07-06.png)

Viola! I can now voice command and conquer!
